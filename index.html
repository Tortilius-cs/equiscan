Cancer Research UK · Convergence Science Centre Prize

AI is changing breast-cancer screening. It needs to work for everyone.

We're five Year 12 students entering the Imperial College Science in Medicine School Teams Prize 2026. Our idea, EquiScan, tackles a problem most people don't know exists: AI screening tools are often less accurate for the women most at risk.

Meet the team See the evidence

The idea, in short

Fix the algorithm. Reach the patient.

Most AI mammography studies barely record who's actually in their data — and Black women, who face a higher risk of aggressive breast cancer, are also less likely to be screened in the first place. EquiScan tackles both halves of that problem at once: a fairer, independently audited algorithm, paired with mobile screening units that reach the communities current services miss.
