What's the idea

Fix the algorithm, reach the patient

A lot of AI mammogram tools are trained mostly on data from White women, so they don't work as well for other groups, even though some groups (like Black women) have higher rates of more dangerous cancer types. On top of that, those same groups are less likely to get screened in the first place. Our idea tries to fix both problems: re-train the AI on more representative data, and bring mobile screening units to the areas that need it most.
